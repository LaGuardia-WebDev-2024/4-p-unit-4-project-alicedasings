Recipe #1: Ginger Snaps!!

  • Time: 35
  • Serves: 24 cookies (but depends on the size you roll them)
Ingredients Quantity
unsalted butter softened 3/4 cup
granulated sugar 1 cup
large egg 1
unsulphured molasses 1/4 cup
all-purpose flour 2 1/2 cups
baking soda 2 tsp
salt (optional) 1/2 tsp
cinnamon 1 tsp
dried ground ginger 1/2 tsp
(optional) sugar to roll cookie balls in

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
Step 2: Mix butter and sugar until fluffy (approx. 2-3 mins)
Step 3: Add egg and molasses, mix well
Step 4: In a seperate bowl, mix flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and ginger
Step 5: Mix both bowls
Step 6: Form balls of dough, (optional-roll balls in granulated sugar). Place on a baking sheet with space between
Step 7: Bake for 10-12 minutes, then remove and allow to cool.

Enjoy!!ü•∞
